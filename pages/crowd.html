Tips for Busy Hours
🚪
Board from the first or last coach — always less crowded than middle coaches.
👩
Ladies coaches are at both ends of every train. Always comfortable for women.
🎒
Keep your bag in front during rush hours to avoid pickpocketing.
🪑
Yellow seats are reserved for senior citizens, pregnant women and differently-abled persons. Please respect this.
📱
Check SahiSafar crowd levels before leaving home — save time, travel comfortably.
